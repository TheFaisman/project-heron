Methodology

It's my firm belief that anyone can learn anything, but only by picking the right learning strategy appropriate to them.

My teaching methodology involves taking the time to understand the learner's current skill and knowledge, then with my own understanding of the destination, I break down the path and journey to bridge the gap for the learner.

Stories, examples, and analogies are the core teaching mechanisms leveraged by Allah throughout The Holy Quran, and I aspire to leverage those same techniques throughout the learning journey.

Practice is the final critical component to cement the learner's new understanding.

Step by step, students will excel past their goals and not only develop new knowledge and understanding, but also the tools required to learn more effectively for the future.
